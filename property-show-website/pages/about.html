Our Story

Accountability Through Every Transaction

First Avenue Properties Ltd is a trusted Kenyan real estate agency providing end-to-end property services — Sales, Letting, Property Management and Advisory — guided by transparency and long-term value.

What sets us apart is public accountability. Through The Property Show with Nancy on K24 TV and Twake Uikaro waku na Muthoni on Kameme TV, our reputation is on air every week — making us answerable to over 1.3 million viewers nationwide.

Over a decade of experience. Clean track record. No association with property fraud. Verifiable processes designed to meet high professional standards.
